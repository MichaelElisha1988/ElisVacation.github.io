Orbi City

About Orbi City Hotel

Orbi City Hotel Official is located on the beachfront in Batumi, a 2-minute walk from Batumi Beach and 3.2 km from Ali and Nino Monument. The property is around 22.5 km from Petra Fortress, 1.8 km from Batumi Archeological Museum and 2.1 km from Aquapark Batumi. The property has a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, an ATM and free WiFi throughout the property.

At the hotel the rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a closet, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, towels and a balcony with a city view. Each room includes an electric tea pot, while certain rooms also offer a kitchenette with a fridge and a stovetop.

Medea Monument is 2.4 km from Orbi City Hotel Official, while The Neptune Fountain is 2.4 km from the property. The nearest airport is Batumi International Airport, 4.3 km from the accommodation.

Orbi Recidents

About Orbi Residence Hotel

Located just a 2-minute walk from Batumi Beach, Orbi Residence Apartment provides accommodations in Batumi with a restaurant, a bar, as well as a 24-hour front desk. This beachfront property has a balcony and free WiFi.

This air-conditioned apartment features 1 bedroom, a satellite flat-screen TV, a dining area, and a kitchen with a fridge and a stovetop. Towels and bed linen are featured in the apartment.

The apartment has a terrace. If you would like to discover the area, hiking is possible nearby and Orbi Residence Apartment can arrange a car rental service.

Ali and Nino Monument is 5.1 km from the accommodation, while Petra Fortress is 25.7 km away. The nearest airport is Batumi International Airport, 3.5 km from Orbi Residence Apartment.
